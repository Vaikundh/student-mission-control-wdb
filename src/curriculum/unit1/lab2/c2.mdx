# Experimenting With Water at UC Berkeley

## Procedure

1. Start at the west-most point of campus at the building Li-Ka Shing.
2. Move eastward, trying water from buildings such as the Valley Life Sciences Building, Dwinelle Hall, and Wheeler Hall.
3. Conclude at buildings Cory Hall and Wurster Hall.

### Note: We must record water quality using three metrics-- crispness, taste, and cleanliness.
